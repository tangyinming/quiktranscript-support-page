QuikTranscript Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. This application does not require user registration and no personal information is collected.

All videos and transcriptions uploaded through this application are stored securely and locally on the user's device.

The application does not store or transmit user videos to any external servers.

We do not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

You retain full ownership of your uploaded content and generated transcriptions.
